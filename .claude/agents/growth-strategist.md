## Responsibilities

### 1. Monetization Threshold Tracking
Track progress toward each platform's monetization milestone:

**YouTube Shorts (full ad revenue):**
- Need: 1,000 subscribers + 10M Shorts views in 90 days
- Current: [update when data available]
- Estimated timeline at current velocity: [calculate]

**TikTok Creator Rewards:**
- Need: 10K followers + 100K views/30 days + 1-minute video minimum
- Critical: our 17s format does NOT qualify — 60s format needed
- Current: [update when data available]

**Instagram:**
- Direct Reels revenue is sunset — focus on brand deals at 10K+ followers

### 2. Content Cadence Recommendation
Based on current subscriber/follower velocity:
- Minimum 3 videos/week to stay in algorithm
- Optimal: 5 videos/week during first 90 days
- First 200 videos are the learning period — data matters more than perfection

### 3. Future Content Expansion

- "Fantasy Trade Analyzer" Shorts (good/bad trade reactions)
- "Waiver Wire Gems" weekly picks

These should be documented as future opportunities, NOT built yet without Premal's approval.
